Cryptocurrencies & Their Creators

Popular Cryptocurrencies

Bitcoin (BTC)

Created by: Satoshi Nakamoto

Bitcoin is the first and most widely used cryptocurrency.

Ethereum (ETH)

Created by: Vitalik Buterin

Ethereum enables smart contracts and decentralized applications.

Binance Coin (BNB)

Created by: Changpeng Zhao

BNB is the native token of the Binance exchange.

Ripple (XRP)

Created by: Chris Larsen & Jed McCaleb

XRP is a cryptocurrency designed for fast and low-cost cross-border payments.

Cardano (ADA)

Created by: Charles Hoskinson

Cardano is a blockchain platform with a strong focus on sustainability and scalability.

Solana (SOL)

Created by: Anatoly Yakovenko

Solana is a high-performance blockchain known for its speed and low transaction fees.
